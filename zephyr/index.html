About Zephyr

A dynamic, female-fronted rock'n'soul band revisiting classics from the 70's onwards, including Fleetwood Mac, Janis Joplin, Nina Simone, Pat Benatar, Stevie Nicks, Anastacia, Toto, and REM.

Available for Bars, Clubs, Weddings, Functions.
