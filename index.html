Experience

In the summer of 2014, I graduated from Newcastle University  with a second-class honours degree in Computer Science. Read more 

At Newcastle, I studied several modules, including Computer Games Development, Object-Oriented Programming, and Server-Side Web Management.

2012-2013 Logica

During my degree studies, I spent a year in industry working for the IT consulting firm Logica (now CGI) . At Logica, I worked as a business and test analyst within the Financial Services sector, developing business scenarios for the client, and designing development criteria for our team of off-shore developers.

2014-Present CGI

I joined CGI as a graduate in the summer of 2014, and began working in various roles in the Government sector, including automation and performance testing. Read more&;

My first role at CGI as a graduate was as a systems integration tester. My job involved writing new scripts in Enterprise Tester, liaising with the client's business analysts, and synchronising our testing to match the needs of the business. After six months in this role, I was promoted to team lead, and led a team of four staff to develop a test suite for a section of the system.

After my spell as a team lead, I changed gears and moved into automation testing. In this team, I developed new skills in using JBoss and Selenium to write and execute new automation scripts in order to enhance the existing regression testing suite.

Once the regression pack for the current release was completed, I moved roles again into the performance team. Here, I learned about load-testing, and recorded and wrote several baseline test scripts using JMeter.
